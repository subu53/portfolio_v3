About

I’m a Data Analyst and aspiring Data Scientist who enjoys the full stack of data work: cleaning and exploration, KPI reporting, dashboards, statistical reasoning, and machine learning.

What I do as a Data Analyst

  • KPI dashboards: define metrics, build dashboards, and track performance over time.
  • Reporting & insights: convert messy data into clean summaries and actionable recommendations.
  • SQL + Excel workflows: query, transform, and validate data reliably.
  • Stakeholder communication: present results clearly with charts, narrative, and business impact.

What I do as a Data Scientist / ML Engineer

  • Predictive modeling: regression/classification with leakage-safe validation.
  • Feature engineering: domain-driven features + rigorous ablations.
  • Interpretability: SHAP/LIME explanations for trust and adoption.
  • Deployable demos: Streamlit apps + GitHub-ready documentation.
My working style

I prefer clean baselines first, then disciplined iteration: better features, better validation, careful tuning, and strong reporting. The goal is not just a score — it’s reliable performance you can explain.
