About Me

Analytical thinking with practical execution.

I am building my career at the intersection of data analysis, data science, and machine learning engineering. My work focuses on understanding the problem, exploring the data carefully, building strong baselines, improving with evidence, and packaging the final result clearly.

I enjoy work that combines business insight, experimentation, predictive modeling, and presentation. Whether it is a dashboard, a forecasting model, or an applied AI prototype, I aim to make the output useful and believable.
